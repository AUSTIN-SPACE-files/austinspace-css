FAQ

Common questions

What if I need more than one page later?

The full £500 comes off a Custom Build if you upgrade within 18 months. Nothing gets thrown away either: moving from Basic to Core is a plan change on the same site, not a rebuild, so your domain, content and form all carry across and the page you started with becomes the homepage.

Do I actually own it?

Yes. The Squarespace subscription is in your name and paid by you from the day it goes live. If you ever want to take it elsewhere, or hand it to someone else, you can. Nothing is held hostage.

What if I need something changed?

Email or text me. Small changes are £45, which covers up to half an hour and is most things. Anything bigger gets a price before I start. There is no monthly fee and no contract.

Can I have a blog?

Squarespace can turn one on, so yes if you want it. Honest answer though: a blog only helps if you actually post, and an abandoned one dated eighteen months ago does more harm than not having one at all. If you are serious about publishing regularly, that is usually the point where a Custom Build makes more sense.

Why Squarespace and not something cheaper?

Because it stays up and stays current without anyone having to maintain it. No security updates to chase, no plugins to break. You pay Squarespace directly, around £11 a month, and that is the only ongoing cost.

How long does it really take?

About a week from the point your form comes back. The thing that slows it down is never the build, it is waiting on words and photographs.
