More about me

Built around your business

Templates and DIY builders give you a starting point. They don't give you a site that's been thought through for what your business actually does, or where it's going. That takes sitting down and getting to know the business properly. The customers, the goals, the awkward corners. Then designing around it.

Built to be found

Most of what makes a site findable is invisible. URL structure, schema markup, image weight, alt text, the order things load in. Built right from day one, this is what gets you found in Google and AI search. Bolted on later, it's expensive and retroactive.

Built to last

The site is built on Squarespace because Squarespace stays out of the way. No security patches to chase. No plugin conflicts to debug. No annual rebuild because the platform you launched on isn't the platform you're running on. The work goes into the site, not into keeping it from falling over.

Agency prices don't match the output

Agency prices include the apparatus around the work. Account managers, project managers, handoffs, layers of process. For some projects, that apparatus is absolutely essential. For most SME sites, it's a good chunk of the ongoing costs.
